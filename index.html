Griz: A Man of the People

Photo by Andre Benz on Unsplash

Ever since Grant Kwiecinski stepped into the electronic scene back in 2011, he completely flipped the community upside down with his effortless crossover between a multitude of genres. As a Detroit native, Kwiecinski grew up with a passion for motown and funk music. He has combined state-of-the-art audio production technology with his trusty saxophone to create his own unique image and brand -- Griz. Today, Kwiecinski has garnered a massive following and sells out nearly every show on tour. His most recent album, Ride Waves, was critically acclaimed for its fresh sound and reinvention of electro-funk.

This page was created by Beau Goergen as a tribute to Griz. Goergen followed Griz in high school and college, seeing him more than 10 times. The music has brought him memories and friendships that will last a lifetime.
